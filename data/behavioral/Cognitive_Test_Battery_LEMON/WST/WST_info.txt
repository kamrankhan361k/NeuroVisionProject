WST
Wortschatztest – vocabulary

Reference: 
Schmidt, K.-H., Metzler, P. (1992): Wortschatztest (WST). Beltz Test GmbH

task:
total of 42 word sets with each one real/target word and five distractors/pseudo words
participant has to identify the one target word 
NO time limit

z-scale (M=0, s=1)
IQ-scale (M=100, s=15)
Z-scale (M=100, s=10)


column names:

WST_1 = WST raw data; how many real words did the pp. recognize correctly
WST_2 = z-scale
WST_3 = IQ-scale
WST_4 = Z-scale
WST_5 = comments
