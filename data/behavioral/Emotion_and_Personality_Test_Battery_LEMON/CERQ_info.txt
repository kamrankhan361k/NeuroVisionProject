CERQ
Cognitive Emotion Regulation Questionnaire

References
1. Garnefski, N., Kraaij, V., & Spinhoven, P. (2001). Negative life events, cognitive emotion regulation and emotional problems. Personality and Individual differences, 30(8), 1311-1327. doi:10.1016/S0191-8869(00)00113-6
2. Loch, N., Hiller, W., & Witthoeft, M. (2011). The Cognitive Emotion Regulation Questionnaire (CERQ). Psychometric evaluation of a German adaptation. Zeitschrift für Klinische Psychologie und Psychotherapie, 40(2), 94-106. DOI: 10.1026/1616-3443/a000079


Timeframe
Trait (im Algemeinen denken)


27 Items

Response scales:
from 1 (almost never) to 5 (almost always)

0 = (fast) nie
1 = manchmal
2 = regelmäßig
3 = häufig
4 = fast immer

Subscales/Columns:	
CERQ_SelfBlame: Self Blame	
CERQ_Acceptance: Acceptance	
CERQ_Rumination: Rumination	
CERQ_positiveRefocusing: Positive Refocusing	
CERQ_RefocusOnPlanning: Refocus On Planning	
CERQ_PositiveReappraisal: Positive Reappraisal	
CERQ_PuttingIntoPerspective: Putting Into Perspective	
CERQ_Catastrophizing: Catastrophizing		
CERQ_BlamingOthers: Blaming Others
