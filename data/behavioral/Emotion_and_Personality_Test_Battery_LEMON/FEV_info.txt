FEV
Fragebogen zum Essverhalten

References:
1. Stunkard, A. J., & Messick, S. (1985). The three-factor eating questionnaire to measure dietary restraint, disinhibition and hunger. Journal of psychosomatic research, 29(1), 71-83. doi:10.1016/0022-3999(85)90010-8
2. Pudel, D. & Westenhöfer, J. (1989). Fragebogen zum Eßverhalten (FEV). Göttingen: Hogrefe.


Timeframe:
Trait


Answer Codes:
from dichotomous scales (applies, does not apply) to 4-point Likert scales from 1 (always) to 4 (never) or 1 (very much) to 4 (not). From 58-60 are answers to select from the list.





Item 60
multiple choice (many options possible)


Subscales/Columns:
ID	
FEV_KK:	Cognitive Restraint  of eating
FEV_STOER: Disinhibition of Eating	
FEV_HUNGER: Susceptibility to Hunger
