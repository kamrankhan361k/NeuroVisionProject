COPE
Coping Orientations to Problems Experienced (Brief COPE)

References
1. Knoll, N., Rieckmann, N., & Schwarzer, R. (2005). Coping as a mediator between personality and stress outcomes: A longitudinal study with cataract surgery patients. European Journal of Personality, 19, 229-247. doi: 10.1002/per.546
2. Carver, C. S.  (1997).  You want to measure coping but your protocol’s too long:  Consider the Brief COPE. International Journal of Behavioral Medicine, 4, 92-100. 



Timeframe:
Trait (in vergangene unangenehmen oder schwierigen Situation)


28 Items


Response scales:
from 1 (not at all) to 4 (very much). 

1 = überhaupt nicht
2 = ein bißchen
3 = ziemlich
4 = sehr
