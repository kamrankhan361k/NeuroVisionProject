Big-Five of Personality (NEO-FFI)

References:
1. Costa, P.T. & McCrae, R.R. (1989). NEO PI/FFI manual supplement for use with the NEO Personality Inventory and the NEO Five-Factor Inventory. Odessa, Fl.: Psychological Assessment Resources.; Costa, P.T. & McCrae, R.R. (1992). Revised NEO Personality Inventory (NEO PI-R) and NEO Five Factor Inventory (NEO-FFI). Timeframe:
Trait


60 Items

Response scores:
from 0 (strong denial) to 4 (strong approval).

0 = Starke Ablehnung
1 = Ablehnung
2 = Neutral
3 = Zustimmung
4 = Starke Zustimmung

NEOFFI_OpennessForExperiences: Openness For Experiences
